Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

RestForge ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

RestForge does not collect or store personal data on external servers.

All data you create in the app (such as workouts, presets, and settings) is stored locally on your device.

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is securely stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple’s CloudKit services.

We do not have access to your iCloud data.

2. Data Usage

  • Track your workouts, exercises, and rest times
  • Provide reminders and notifications
  • Display information within widgets and Live Activities

We do not use your data for analytics, tracking, or advertising.

3. Notifications

RestForge uses local notifications to remind you about rest periods and workout events. These notifications are generated on your device.

4. Health and Activity Data

If you choose to integrate with Apple Health or Apple Watch, data is only used to enhance your experience and is not shared externally.

5. In-App Purchases

Payments are handled by Apple. We do not collect or store payment information.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Apple iCloud (CloudKit)
  • StoreKit (in-app purchases)
  • Apple HealthKit (optional)
  • iOS/watchOS system frameworks

8. Data Security

Your data is protected by Apple’s systems on your device and iCloud.

9. Your Control

  • Delete workouts and presets anytime
  • Disable notifications
  • Disable iCloud sync
  • Delete the app to remove data

10. Children’s Privacy

RestForge does not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes

This policy may be updated. Changes will be reflected here.
