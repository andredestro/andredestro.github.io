Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

SleepWave ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

SleepWave does not collect or store personal data on external servers owned by us.

All preferences you configure in the app (such as selected sounds, volume levels, EQ modes, and sleep timer settings) are stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers.

We do not have access to any data on your device.

2. Data Usage

  • Play ambient and generated sounds for sleep, focus, and relaxation
  • Apply EQ presets (Deep Sleep, Focus, Relax) to audio playback
  • Schedule and manage the sleep timer
  • Remember your sound and volume preferences during a session

We do not use your data for analytics, tracking, or advertising.

3. Audio Playback

All audio content — including bundled ambient sounds and procedurally generated noise (white, brown, and pink noise) — is processed entirely on your device using Apple's AVFoundation framework. No audio data is sent to external servers at any point.

4. Sleep Timer

The sleep timer (a Premium feature) automatically stops playback after a chosen duration. The timer is managed entirely on-device. No data is sent to external servers.

5. Haptic Feedback

SleepWave uses haptic feedback to enhance interactions such as playing sounds or confirming purchases. Haptics are generated locally by iOS and do not involve any data collection or transmission.

6. In-App Purchases

Payments are handled entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not collect or store any payment or billing information.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties.

8. Third-Party Services

  • StoreKit (in-app purchases)
  • iOS system frameworks (AVFoundation, UIKit)

9. Data Security

Since SleepWave stores no personal data externally, your privacy is inherently protected. Any local preferences are secured by your device's built-in protection features, including device encryption.

10. Your Control

  • Adjust or reset sound and volume preferences at any time within the app
  • Cancel your subscription via iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions
  • Delete the app to permanently remove all local data

11. Children's Privacy

SleepWave does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

12. Changes

This policy may be updated. Changes will be reflected here.
