Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Hydro ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Hydro does not collect or store personal data on external servers owned by us.

All data you create in the app (such as hydration entries, daily goals, and settings) is stored on your device using SwiftData and automatically synced across your devices via your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service.

We do not have access to your iCloud data.

2. Data Usage

  • Track your daily water intake
  • Calculate progress toward your hydration goal
  • Display your history and streaks
  • Provide reminders and notifications
  • Show hydration data within widgets and Apple Watch

We do not use your data for analytics, tracking, or advertising.

3. Notifications

Hydro uses local notifications to remind you to drink water throughout the day. These notifications are scheduled and generated entirely on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

4. Widgets

Hydro provides Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets that display your hydration progress. Widget data is shared between the app and the widget extension using a local App Group container stored on your device.

5. Apple Health

Hydro can optionally write your water intake entries to Apple Health as Dietary Water. This feature is disabled by default and can be enabled or disabled at any time in the app's Settings under "Integrations". No health data is transmitted to our servers — all data flows directly between Hydro and the Health app on your device via Apple's HealthKit framework.

6. Apple Watch

If you use the Apple Watch companion app (Premium feature), your hydration data is shared between your iPhone and Apple Watch using a local App Group container. Data is also synced to your other devices via iCloud/CloudKit using the same mechanism as the main app.

7. In-App Purchases

Payments are handled entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not collect or store any payment or billing information.

8. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties.

9. Third-Party Services

  • Apple iCloud (CloudKit) — cross-device data sync
  • Apple HealthKit — optional Dietary Water logging (opt-in)
  • StoreKit (in-app purchases)
  • iOS system frameworks (WidgetKit, UserNotifications, WatchKit)

10. Data Security

Your data is stored on your device and in your personal iCloud account, protected by Apple's built-in security features including device encryption and iCloud end-to-end encryption where applicable.

11. Your Control

  • Delete individual hydration entries anytime
  • Disable reminders in the app settings
  • Reset your daily goal and preferences at any time
  • Enable or disable Apple Health sync in Settings → Integrations
  • Revoke HealthKit access via iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → Hydro
  • Disable iCloud sync via iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Hydro
  • Delete iCloud data via iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage
  • Delete the app to permanently remove all local data

12. Children's Privacy

Hydro does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
